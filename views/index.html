About Us

Nkhoma University was successfully registered by the Registrarâ€™s office of the Government of Malawi in 2011. It is a private Christian University with a constitution and charter.

The University is owned by the Church of Central Africa Presbyterian (CCAP) of the Nkhoma Synod. It is open to students of all faiths, and respects academic freedom and freedom of thought and expression within the basic tenets of the Christian faith.

The establishment of this university was largely premised on the need to advance Christianity, education and health to the tertiary level.
